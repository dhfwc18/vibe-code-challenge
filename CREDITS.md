# Third-Party Credits

All assets, libraries, and reference data used in 20-50 are listed here.
Every entry must be added before the item is committed to the repository.

Format per entry:
  Name:    display name
  Type:    Library | Font | Image | Audio | Data | Other
  Source:  URL or publication reference
  Licence: licence identifier
  Use:     what it is used for in the project

## Reference Data

Name:    HM Treasury Green Book (2022)
Type:    Data

Licence: Open Government Licence v3.0
Use:     Social discount rate (STPR), appraisal methodology, whole-economy cost
         reference for net zero transition (GBP 321bn net, 2020-2050)

Name:    DESNZ Valuation of Energy Use and Greenhouse Gas Emissions (November 2023)
Type:    Data
Source:  Department for Energy Security and Net Zero, UK Government
Licence: Open Government Licence v3.0
Use:     Shadow Price of Carbon table (GBP 241-378/tCO2e central, 2020-2050),
         technology LCOE cost anchors, historical energy price anchors

Type:    Data
Source:  Climate Change Committee -- https://www.theccc.org.uk/
Licence: Open Government Licence v3.0

Licence: Open Government Licence v3.0
